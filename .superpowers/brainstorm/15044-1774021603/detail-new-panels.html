Key design decisions baked in:

  • Rolling avg on progression = 10-ride window (smooths noise without lagging too much)
  • FTP computed per month from stream data (best 20min × 0.95)
  • Best efforts = 1min, 5min, 20min (neuromuscular, VO2max, threshold)
  • Zone colors follow Coggan standard (matching Activity Detail)
  • Records table has drill-down links to Activity Detail
